Rice Pudding

Description Rice pudding is a simple sweet desert.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup rice
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1/4 teasoon salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. boil 1 1/2 cups of water
  2. add rice and simmer for 20mins
  3. in seperate sauce pan combine 1 1/2 cups cooked rice, 1 1/2 cups milk,sugar and salt
  4. cook over medium heat untill thick and creamy,15 to 20 mins.
  5. remove from heat and stir in butter and vanilla.Enjoy.
